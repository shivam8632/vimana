Optician Website

Project Brief

Vimana Digital had the task of developing a contemporary and responsive optician website for The Duffield Clinic. The new website must prioritise ease of navigation and modern design, while effectively highlighting each service provided. Furthermore, the website should be compatible with mobile devices and tablets. Vimana Digital is granted complete creative freedom for both design and development.

Building the Website

The Duffield Clinic offers a range of services, including eyecare and hearing care. Their website required a clear user journey with intuitive navigation and a straightforward enquiry method. While the client provided content tailored to their services, we assisted by refining the text and managing the majority of the imagery to ensure a cohesive and professional presentation.
