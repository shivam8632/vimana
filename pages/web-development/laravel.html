Why Laravel?

Laravel's MVC architecture supports clear separation between logic and presentation, enhancing performance and allowing for better code organisation. Its extensive ecosystem, including tools like Laravel Nova, Echo, and Horizon, empowers developers to build feature-rich applications efficiently. Laravel’s emphasis on clean, secure code makes it an ideal choice for businesses aiming to build reliable web applications.

What is MVC?

MVC stands for Model-View-Controller. It's a way to organise your code when building websites or apps. Imagine it like a restaurant: The Model is the kitchen where all your data is prepared. The View is the dining area or the presentation of the data, what you see on your plate. The Controller acts as the waiter, taking your order (input) to the kitchen (Model) and bringing back your food (output) to the table (View). This helps keep your code neat and separates the different parts of your app for easier management and updates.
